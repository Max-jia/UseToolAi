## 1. Record Everything. Edit Nothing While Recording.

The biggest time-waster in video editing is editing while recording. You do a take, stop, review, delete, restart. By the time you get a clean recording, you've spent 45 minutes for what should have been 5.

### The fix: record in one take.

- Stumble over a word? Keep going. The AI will help you cut it out later.
- Long pause while you think? Keep the camera rolling. Silence removal handles this.
- Said the same thing twice? Doesn't matter. You'll delete a sentence in the transcript later, not in the timeline.

**One recording. No stopping. No reviewing. Just get the footage.**

## 2. Let AI Remove the Dead Air

Pauses and silences make videos feel slow. Manually cutting them out is tedious. AI tools do it in one click.

### In CapCut (free, no watermark):

1. Import your video
2. Click **Audio** → **Reduce Noise** (one click)
3. Right-click the timeline → **Remove Silences**
4. Adjust the threshold — I use 0.3 seconds. Anything shorter stays. Anything longer gets cut.

CapCut will automatically split your clip at every pause longer than your threshold and delete the gaps. A 20-minute recording becomes 12 minutes with zero manual timeline scrubbing.

### In Descript (free, 1 export/month):

1. Import your video
2. Descript transcribes it automatically
3. Go to **Edit** → **Remove Filler Words** → select "um," "uh," "like," "you know"
4. Click Apply

Not only does Descript remove the pauses — it removes the filler words too. The transcript updates. The video updates. Done.

## 3. Edit Video by Editing Text (The Technique That Changes Everything)

Descript's core feature is the one that will save you the most time: you edit the transcript, and the video follows.

### How it works:

1. Import video → Descript transcribes it into text (takes ~30 seconds for a 10-minute video)
2. Read the transcript like a document
3. Delete sentences you don't want → those sections disappear from the video
4. Copy-paste text to rearrange → the video clips rearrange with it
5. Type new text → Descript generates AI voiceover in your voice (requires paid plan)

**This is faster than timeline editing by an order of magnitude.** You're reading text, not scrubbing through waveforms. Your brain processes text faster than video. The editing decisions happen at the speed of reading.

### Real workflow:

- **Pass 1 (2 minutes):** Read the transcript. Delete entire paragraphs that don't belong.
- **Pass 2 (3 minutes):** Tighten sentences. Delete filler words. Remove repetition.
- **Pass 3 (2 minutes):** Add chapter markers. These become YouTube chapters or podcast segments.

7 minutes of text editing replaces 30 minutes of timeline scrubbing.

## 4. Generate Captions Automatically

Captioned videos get higher watch time and completion rates. Manually typing captions is terrible. AI captioning is nearly perfect in 2026.

### In CapCut (best free option):

1. Click **Text** → **Auto Captions**
2. Select your language
3. Wait ~10 seconds
4. Review for errors (rare with clear audio, maybe 1-2 corrections per 5-minute video)
5. Style the captions — CapCut has templates optimized for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts

### In Descript:

Captions are built into the transcript. Export with burned-in captions or as a separate SRT file. No extra step required — the transcript you edited in Section 3 is the caption file.

## 5. Fix Audio in One Click

Bad audio makes a video unwatchable faster than bad video. AI audio cleanup is now a one-click operation.

### The fix:

- **Descript:** Click **Studio Sound**. That's it. Background noise, echo, and room tone vanish. Your voice sounds like it was recorded in a studio.
- **CapCut:** Click **Audio** → **Enhance Voice**. It's not as good as Descript's Studio Sound, but it's free.
- **Adobe Podcast (free, browser-based):** Upload audio. Click Enhance Speech. Download the cleaned file. Best quality of the free options.

### What it fixes:
- Air conditioner hum
- Keyboard clicking
- Room echo
- Traffic noise
- Mic hiss

None of these require manual EQ adjustment anymore. It's literally one button.

## 6. Export in the Right Format for Each Platform

Different platforms need different formats. Don't export once and upload everywhere — the crop, caption style, and length should change.

### Quick export guide:

| Platform | Aspect Ratio | Max Length | Caption Style |
|----------|-------------|------------|---------------|
| TikTok | 9:16 (vertical) | 10 min | Large, animated, center-bottom |
| Reels | 9:16 (vertical) | 90 sec | Same as TikTok |
| Shorts | 9:16 (vertical) | 60 sec | Clean, minimal |
| YouTube | 16:9 (horizontal) | Unlimited | Smaller, bottom-third |
| LinkedIn | 1:1 or 16:9 | 15 min | Burned-in, accessible |

**CapCut** has one-click export templates for each platform. **Descript** lets you export multiple formats from one project — edit once, export for every platform.

## The 15-Minute Workflow

Timer starts after recording:

| Minute | Action | Tool |
|--------|--------|------|
| 0:00-0:30 | Import video. AI transcribes. | Descript (or CapCut) |
| 0:30-1:00 | Remove silences and filler words. | Descript: Edit → Remove Filler Words |
| 1:00-4:00 | Read transcript. Delete bad sections. Tighten sentences. | Descript text editor |
| 4:00-5:00 | Add chapter markers. | Descript: type `/` to add markers |
| 5:00-5:15 | One-click audio cleanup. | Descript: Studio Sound |
| 5:15-5:45 | Review auto-generated captions. Fix 1-2 errors. | Built into transcript |
| 5:45-7:00 | Add intro/outro if needed. Add background music. | CapCut or Descript stock library |
| 7:00-8:00 | Export for primary platform. | Descript → Export |
| 8:00-10:00 | Resize and re-export for other platforms. | CapCut auto-reframe |

**10 minutes of editing for a final product that would have taken an hour manually.**

## FAQ

### Q: Can I really edit entirely in Descript's free tier?

**A:** You can do everything except export more than once per month. The free tier is for testing the workflow. If you produce videos regularly, the $16/month Hobbyist plan pays for itself in the first hour of saved editing time.

### Q: Is CapCut safe to use given it's owned by ByteDance?

**A:** CapCut is owned by ByteDance (TikTok's parent company). If data privacy is a concern, use it offline (desktop version) and block its network access. For sensitive content, use Descript or DaVinci Resolve instead.

### Q: Does AI editing work for non-English videos?

**A:** Yes. Descript supports 23 languages for transcription. CapCut supports 20+ languages for captions. Accuracy varies — English is nearly perfect, other major languages are very good, less common languages have more errors.
