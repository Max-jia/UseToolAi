Three AI tools now compete to replace your search workflow. They work differently enough that picking the wrong one wastes time and produces bad answers.

Perplexity finds facts and cites sources. ChatGPT Search synthesizes and writes. Gemini searches your Google data alongside the web.

---

## Perplexity

Perplexity answers every question with inline citations. Each claim links to a source. You can verify anything it says.

Pro costs $20 per month and includes model choice: route queries to GPT-5.5, Claude Opus, Gemini, or Sonar. Deep Research runs on Claude Opus 4.5 and shows you a research plan before executing. You can edit the plan.

Free gives you 5 Pro searches per day. Enough to test. Not enough for real work.

Max at $200 per month adds Model Council, which runs your query across three frontier models simultaneously and synthesizes the results. Also adds Perplexity Computer with 400-plus tool integrations and the Comet AI-native browser.

The weakness: Perplexity summarizes what sources say. It does not synthesize deeply the way ChatGPT does. You get sourced facts. You do not always get deep analysis.

Pick Perplexity when you need verifiable information. Research papers. Market analysis. Fact-checking. Anything where being able to trace every claim to a source matters.

---

## ChatGPT Search

ChatGPT Search lives inside ChatGPT. You activate it and it searches the web in real time. Results show inline with sources.

ChatGPT Plus at $20 per month includes Search, Deep Research, image generation, and Codex. No separate subscription. One tool that does five things.

Deep Research produces 10-to-20-page reports with citations. It runs autonomously, searching dozens of sources and synthesizing them. Each report takes 5 to 10 minutes but the output is genuinely useful.

The weakness: ChatGPT Search is a feature bolted onto a chat app. It works well but the interface is not built for research workflows the way Perplexity is. No focus modes. No academic filter. You search and you chat. That is the experience.

Pick ChatGPT Search when you already use ChatGPT for everything else and you want one tool instead of two. Also pick it for Deep Research reports. No other tool produces synthesized research documents with the same depth and readability.

---

## Gemini Deep Research

Gemini Deep Research searches Google's index. It pulls from more sources than Perplexity or ChatGPT. It also searches your Google Workspace: Gmail, Drive, Docs.

Gemini Advanced costs $19.99 per month bundled with 2TB Google Drive, YouTube Premium Lite, and Google Health. The best bundle value among all research tools.

Deep Research output quality trails Perplexity and ChatGPT. The sources are comprehensive. The writing reads robotic. Users consistently report needing more editing passes.

The killer feature is workspace integration. Ask Gemini a question and it searches your email, your documents, and the web simultaneously. No other tool can do this.

Pick Gemini Deep Research when you live in Google Workspace and you need AI that searches both the internet and your own files.

---

## Which One

Perplexity gives you the most verifiable answers. Every claim linked to a source. Pick it for fact-based research.

ChatGPT gives you the deepest synthesis. Deep Research reports that read like commissioned analysis. Pick it when you need to understand a topic deeply, not just collect facts.

Gemini gives you the best bundle value and workspace integration. Pick it when you want one subscription that covers AI, storage, YouTube, and email search.
