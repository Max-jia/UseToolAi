Midjourney, DALL-E 4, and Leonardo AI serve different people with different needs. You cannot pick the "best" one. You can pick the one that matches how you work.

---

## Midjourney V7

Midjourney cares about one thing: making images that look beautiful.

V7 achieves a 90% keeper rate for fantasy and concept art. The aesthetic quality is unmatched. V7 was a complete architecture rebuild — fewer artifacts, better composition, richer lighting. 8K upscaling with 300% more texture detail.

Relax Mode on the Standard plan at $30/month gives effectively unlimited generations at slower speeds.

The trade-off: text rendering succeeds about 10% of the time. No free tier exists. No public API exists. Third-party resellers like EvoLink offer per-request access at a few cents per image.

Pick Midjourney when visual quality matters most and you do not need text in your images.

---

## DALL-E 4 (GPT Image)

DALL-E does what Midjourney cannot: it renders readable text.

Logos, posters, and social graphics with embedded text are DALL-E's strength. The ChatGPT integration lets you iterate through conversation. Ask for adjustments. Refine details. The process feels natural.

Pick DALL-E when your designs need working text, or when you already use ChatGPT and want integrated image generation.

---

## Leonardo AI

Leonardo does more things than the other two combined. 80-plus models under one roof. Custom LoRA training at $12/month. 3D texture generation for game developers. Consistent Character Engine for maintaining faces across scenes. The most generous free tier at 150 tokens per day.

Canva acquired Leonardo in 2024. The Essential plan comes included with Canva Business.

Pick Leonardo when you want the best value, the most features, or you build game assets.
