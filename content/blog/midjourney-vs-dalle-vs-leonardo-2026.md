## The Verdict by Use Case

**Choose Midjourney if:** Artistic beauty is your top priority. V7 delivers a 90% keeper rate for fantasy/concept art. The aesthetic quality is unmatched, but you'll need other tools for text-heavy designs.

**Choose DALL-E 4 if:** You need AI images with readable text. Logos, posters, and social graphics with embedded text are DALL-E's superpower. The ChatGPT integration makes iterative refinement effortless.

**Choose Leonardo AI if:** You want the best value and versatility. 80+ models, custom LoRA training at $12/mo, and the most generous free tier. Game developers love the 3D texture generation and consistent character engine.

**Pro tip from Reddit:** "Midjourney for the wow factor. DALL-E when I need text to actually work. Leonardo when I'm iterating fast and don't want to worry about credits."
