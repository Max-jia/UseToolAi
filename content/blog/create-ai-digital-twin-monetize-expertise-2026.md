## Step 1: Collect Your Content

The AI is only as smart as what you feed it. Gather everything you have ever published on your topic.

**Sources to collect:**
- YouTube videos (your own or interviews you've done)
- Podcast appearances
- Blog posts and articles
- Course materials
- Email newsletters
- Twitter/X threads where you shared expertise

**How to extract YouTube transcripts:**
1. Open your video on YouTube
2. Click the three dots below the video → Show transcript
3. Click the toggle to show full transcript
4. Select all, copy, paste into a .txt file
5. Name the file after the video title

Aim for at least 10 sources. More is better. The AI searches across all of them when answering questions.

---

## Step 2: Build the AI in Mind Studio

Mind Studio (youai.ai) is a free platform for building AI applications. No coding. Drag and drop.

1. Sign up at youai.ai
2. Click **Create New AI** → Choose **AI Character** template
3. Upload all your text files into one **Data Source**

**Critical step:** Put everything in one data source. Not one per file. The AI searches across all of them when answering. Separate data sources means it can only search one at a time.

---

## Step 3: Write the System Instructions

This is the prompt that defines how your AI behaves. Here is a template:

```
## Identity
- Name: [YOUR AI'S NAME]
- Role: AI consultant trained on [YOUR NAME]'s expertise in [TOPIC]
- Based exclusively on the provided content. Never fabricate.

## Knowledge
- Trained on [YOUR NAME]'s interviews, articles, and courses
- Covers: [LIST YOUR TOPIC AREAS]

## Tone
- [YOUR TONE]: direct, practical, no fluff
- Answers in [YOUR NAME]'s voice and perspective
- Uses specific examples from the source material
- Admits when it doesn't know something
```

## Step 4: Set Up the Automation Workflow

Mind Studio uses a visual workflow builder. Connect these modules:

```
[Collect user question] → [AI expands the question for better search]
→ [Query your knowledge base] → [Generate answer] → [Display to user]
```

**The expansion step is important.** If someone asks "how do I grow on YouTube," the AI expands this to "what strategies did [YOUR NAME] use to grow their YouTube channel, what content approaches worked, what mistakes did they make" before searching. This dramatically improves answer quality.

## Step 5: Set Your Price

Mind Studio lets you charge for access. Connect Stripe in the Publishing tab. Set a price.

**Pricing models that work:**

| Model | Price | Best For |
|-------|-------|----------|
| One-time access | $19-49 | General audience, low commitment |
| Monthly subscription | $10-30/month | Ongoing value, recurring revenue |
| Free (lead magnet) | $0 | Build email list, upsell consulting |

Start free. Prove people use it. Add a paid tier once you have testimonials and usage data.

## FAQ

### Q: Can I create a twin of someone else's expertise?

**A:** Only with permission. Using someone else's content without consent to build a commercial AI is a legal minefield. Build a twin of your own expertise, or get explicit permission.

### Q: How is this different from a Custom GPT?

**A:** Custom GPTs live inside ChatGPT and compete with millions of others. Mind Studio lets you create a standalone AI with its own interface, branding, and payment integration. You control the experience.

### Q: What if my AI gives bad advice?

**A:** Add a disclaimer: "This AI is trained on [YOUR NAME]'s content and provides general guidance. It is not a substitute for professional advice." Every AI consultant should have this.
