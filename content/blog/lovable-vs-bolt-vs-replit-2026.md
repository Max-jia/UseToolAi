Three AI app builders. All three let you describe an app in plain English and get working code. All three are growing fast. All three serve different people.

Lovable generates polished SaaS apps with Supabase backends and Stripe payments. Bolt.new spits out working prototypes in the browser in seconds. Replit runs 50-plus programming languages in a real cloud IDE.

---

## Lovable

Lovable builds React and TypeScript apps with Tailwind styling. Describe your idea. Get a full-stack app with database, authentication, and Stripe payments.

Supabase handles the backend. GitHub sync pushes clean code to your repo. You can export and continue building in Cursor or VS Code.

Pro costs $25 per month. Complex apps burn credits fast. Realistic monthly spend runs $25 to $75 with top-ups. Business at $50 adds SSO and team workspaces.

Lovable hit $200 million ARR faster than any SaaS company in history. Eight months from launch. That speed says more about product-market fit than any review.

Pick Lovable when you are a non-technical founder who needs a polished SaaS MVP in days, not weeks. The output looks professional. The backend works. You own the code.

---

## Bolt.new

Bolt.new runs entirely in your browser. Type a prompt. Watch a full-stack app appear. No setup. No local environment. No waiting for anything to install.

WebContainers technology runs Node.js natively inside the browser. Instant npm installs. Instant preview. Supports React, Next.js, Vue, Svelte, Angular, Astro, and Remix.

Pro costs $25 per month for 10 million tokens. Tokens roll over for one month. Pro 100 at $100 gives 100 million tokens.

Bolt.new hit $40 million ARR in five months. The open-source engine means you can self-host or customize.

Pick Bolt.new when you need to test an idea in 60 seconds. Rapid prototyping. Hackathon projects. Quick experiments you are not sure will work.

---

## Replit

Replit is a real cloud IDE. It runs Python, Go, Java, Rust, and 47 other languages. Built-in hosting, database, and authentication. SOC 2 Type II certified.

Agent 4 launched in March 2026 with autonomous app generation and real-browser testing. Design Mode turns mockups into interactive prototypes before you write code.

Core costs $20 to $25 per month. Pro at $95 to $100 adds the strongest models and 15 collaborators. Teams at $40 per user with SSO.

Real monthly cost for active builders runs $50 to $150. The agent can loop on bugs and burn credits. The complexity of a full IDE can overwhelm beginners.

Pick Replit when you need Python or Go backends. Multi-language projects. Enterprise compliance. Or you want a real IDE, not a prompt-to-app box.
