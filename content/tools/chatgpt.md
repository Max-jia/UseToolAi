ChatGPT, developed by OpenAI, is the dominant AI assistant with over 300 million weekly active users as of mid-2026. It pioneered the conversational AI category and continues to lead in feature breadth — no other platform combines chat, image generation, coding agents, deep research, and voice interaction in a single subscription.

## Pricing Reality (2026 Update)

OpenAI now offers 7 tiers. The **Plus plan at $20/month** remains the sweet spot — it's held this price for over 3 years while adding major features. The Free tier now shows ads (as of February 2026 in the US), and a new $8/month Go tier offers higher limits but still includes ads. The Pro tiers ($100-200/month) target power users who need unlimited access to the most capable models.

A credit-based system allows Plus and Pro users to purchase additional capacity when plan limits are reached, covering Codex usage, ChatGPT for Excel, and premium model access.

## Real User Sentiment

According to G2 (4.7/5 across 2,600+ reviews) and Capterra (4.4/5 across 375+ reviews), users consistently praise ChatGPT's versatility and ease of use. The most common complaints involve hallucinations (requiring fact-checking), pricing complexity across 7 tiers, and generic writing patterns that need careful prompting to overcome.

## The Bottom Line

ChatGPT is the default choice for most users because it covers 80% of AI needs in one tool. For focused tasks — long document analysis (Claude), citation-heavy research (Perplexity), or budget use (DeepSeek) — specialized alternatives may be better. But for general-purpose AI assistance, nothing matches ChatGPT's breadth and ease of use.
